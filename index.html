Neil Magnuson is an American actor based in New York City. Born and raised in a small town in Kansas, he came to New York straight out of high school to study at NYU's Tisch School of the Arts, earning a BFA in Acting. He promptly quit acting and pursued a second education: tending bar, running live sound, and encouraging miscreants to leave, often all at once. After a particularly bad dustup, he began to think acting was a better way to make a living, which he has for a number of years. He brings all this experience and plenty more into performance, whether it be stage, film, or playing in a badass honky tonk band.
